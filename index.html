Dr. Feng Wang received her B.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from Wuhan University in June 2003 and June 2008, respectively. Currently she is an associate professor of School of Computer Science in Wuhan University. From 2006 to 2008, she was a visiting student at Department of Computer Science of City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Professor Xiaotie Deng. And she continue her research with Professor Deng as a senior research assistant at City University of Hong Kong in 2010. From 2011 to 2012, she was doing postdoctoral research with Professor Ling Liu in the College of Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology.

Her current research interests mainly include Machine Learning and Computational Intelligence. More specifically, she is interested in: Multi/Many Objective Optimization, Estimation of Distribution Algorithms and their applications in Financial/Economic problems; Deep Learning and its application in Image Processing.
